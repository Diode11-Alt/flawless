# How Employers Register with NAFIS in the UAE

NAFIS is the UAE's national public employment service. Registering your company and your employees with NAFIS is a mandatory legal requirement for all employers in the UAE.

## Registering as an Employer
Before you can hire anyone, you must obtain an Employer (PE) Number from the Commissioner for Revenue. Once you have this, you must register your company with NAFIS.
- **Required Details:** Company registration number, VAT number, and the PE number.

## Registering Employees
Every time you hire a new employee, you must submit an Engagement Form to NAFIS on their first day of work.
- **Information Needed:** Employee's ID card or Passport number, social security number, job title, and start date.
- **Terminations:** Similarly, a Termination Form must be submitted when an employee leaves.
