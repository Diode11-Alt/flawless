# Understanding Probation Periods and Termination Rules Under UAE Employment Law

Navigating the legalities of employment termination in the UAE is crucial for maintaining compliance and a positive employer brand.

## Probation Periods
By default, UAE employment contracts carry a 6-month probation period.
- **Exceptions:** For highly technical or executive roles, this can be extended up to 1 year if agreed upon in writing. Shorter periods apply for short-term contracts.
- **Termination During Probation:** Either party can terminate the employment without providing a reason, provided they give one week's notice (if the employee has been employed for more than one month).

## Termination Post-Probation
Terminating an employee after probation requires a "good and sufficient cause" or due to redundancy.
- **Notice Periods:** Notice periods are statutory and depend on the length of continuous service (ranging from 1 week to up to 12 weeks).
- **Severance:** Statutory severance applies primarily in cases of redundancy.
