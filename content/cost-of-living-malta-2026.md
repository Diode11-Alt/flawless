# Cost of Living in the UAE for Relocating Professionals (2026)

Before accepting a job offer in the UAE, it's essential to understand the local cost of living to ensure your salary aligns with your lifestyle expectations.

## Accommodation
Rent is the largest expense.
- **1-Bedroom Apartment (Central hubs like Dubai):** €900 - €1,300/month.
- **1-Bedroom Apartment (Outside center):** €700 - €900/month.
- **Utilities (Water, Electricity, Internet):** €100 - €150/month.

## Food and Groceries
- **Groceries:** Approximately €250 - €400/month for a single person, depending on reliance on imported goods.
- **Dining Out:** A meal at an inexpensive restaurant costs around €15 - €20.

## Transportation
- **Public Transport:** The Tallinja card offers capped, affordable bus travel (often free for residents).
- **Taxis/Ride-Hailing:** A typical short trip costs €10 - €15.
