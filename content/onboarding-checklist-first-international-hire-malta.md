# Onboarding Checklist for Employers Hiring Their First International Employee in the UAE

Bringing your first international hire to the UAE is an exciting milestone, but it requires careful coordination. Use this checklist to ensure nothing is missed.

## Pre-Arrival
- [ ] **Work Permit:** Secure the Employment Visa or EU Blue Card via Identity the UAE.
- [ ] **Employment Contract:** Ensure the contract complies with UAE labor law (DIER regulations).
- [ ] **Housing:** Arrange temporary accommodation for the first 2-4 weeks.
- [ ] **Flights:** Book and coordinate arrival logistics.

## First Week
- [ ] **NAFIS Registration:** Submit the Engagement Form on day one.
- [ ] **Social Security & Tax:** Register the employee for a Social Security number and a tax number.
- [ ] **Bank Account:** Provide the necessary employer reference letters for bank account opening.
- [ ] **Health & Safety:** Conduct mandatory workplace orientation.
