# Dubai Emiratisation (Tawteen) Guide 2026: What Employers Need to Know

The UAE's Emiratisation (Tawteen) initiative is a critical regulatory framework aimed at integrating UAE Nationals into the private sector. As of 2026, the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation (MOHRE) has updated its targets and compliance mechanisms.

## Understanding the New Targets

For companies with 50 or more employees, the annual Emiratisation growth target remains a priority. The penalties for non-compliance are substantial, but more importantly, integrating Emirati talent offers strategic advantages in understanding the local market.

- **Career Development Plans:** Provide clear pathways for progression to ensure high retention rates among Emirati staff.
